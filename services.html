Recruitment Support Services

Strategic preparation and recruitment intelligence modeled exactly for businesses seeking to hire from the Philippines.

We operate as a professional consulting extension to your HR department, making the complex Philippine hiring framework structured, sequential, and predictable.

A

Employer Hiring Preparation

We work with you to analyze your hiring goals and map out the entire structural preparation required before recruitment can formally begin. This early-stage alignment is vital to prevent future recruitment bottlenecks.

B

Employer Documentation Guidance

The Philippine government has strict policies for foreign employer accreditation. We guide you precisely on how your company documents, contracts, and internal policies must be aligned to satisfy the authorities.

C

Recruitment Coordination Support

We orchestrate communications between your company and the prospective candidate pool. We offer strategic support that bridges any cultural or procedural gaps between what an overseas organization expects and what candidate pipelines look like in the Philippines.

D

Worker Processing Support

Once a candidate is selected, we maintain oversight on deployment processing and compliance checking. We ensure you are kept informed as candidates complete medicals, training, and embassy visits.

E

End-to-End Recruitment Support

PRVS delivers continuity. Rather than navigating the complex journey alone, we serve as your anchor from day one of preparation until the worker physically deploys. This is achieved through unified coordination with a licensed agency.

Important Legal Notice: PRVS operates as a professional support and advisory service. Official recruitment activities, including placement and contractual processing, are coordinated strictly through a licensed Philippine recruitment agency based in the Philippines.
